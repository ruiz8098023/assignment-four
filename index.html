Just the Best in Video Games

Here are the best in the world of video gaming...or at least that's what I've seen. I'm not a noob, I swear.

Super Smash Bros Mains

Kirby

A pink ameoba, this character can copy his opponent's abilities and use it against them. Also, he's super cute.

Meta Knight

Did you know the english dub selected for Meta Knight in the show "Kirby: Right Back At Ya!" was a Spanish/Transelvanian accent? Cool, right?

Toon Link

From Wind Waker, (another game I never played) this character has a great bow and arrow you can spam throughout the fight.

Mario Kart Vehicles

I've used these and won. Somehow. Maybe it's because I practiced more than the average adult, even though I don't own a Switch. Maybe it's Mabelline.

I could've done a flip-card thing where each image clicked shows the kart name and stats, but I'm not that productive. So here's the run down.

Landship

  • Acceleration is pretty high up there with this boat on land.
  • Ground and Water handling is also pretty good. Because it's a boat.
  • Feel like a pirate of the roads.

Streetle

  • An Animal Crossing original vehicle.
  • Acceleration is great, so you can pretend like you didn't just slip on a banana peel.
  • Looks like a beetle. Is light like a beetle.

Pipe Frame

  • More of an average kart, but for the guys that like that body.
  • Did you ever wonder what it would be like to control that old Windows screen saver?
